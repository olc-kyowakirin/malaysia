Kyowa Kirin Malaysia

We help patients live better lives, using the power of innovation in life sciences and technologies.


Since the early 2000s, Kyowa Kirin has been present in Malaysia with our innovative medicines designed to alleviate suffering and restore health to patients. As part of the larger global Kyowa Kirin Group headquartered in Japan, we are dedicated to pursuing our Commitment to Life as an integral part of our work.

From humble beginnings and faltering first steps, we have progressed steadily over the years to become a recognized name in the Malaysian pharmaceutical scene. As always, we remain determined to continue forging ahead with a rich pipeline and new therapeutic areas to venture into, all the while conducting ourselves with the highest standard of ethics and quality assurance.
